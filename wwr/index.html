Our purpose is give you a soaked-to-the-skin, adrenaline-charged action. Or it can be a more tranquil activity, immersed in wildlife, landscapes and history. Whatever type of white water rafting experience you're seeking, Our company has the perfect adventure for you.

We provide the basics of equipment for Rafting such as paddle, a personal flotation device and a helmet. We also offer a number of other pieces of equipment such as Wetsuits and Paddling Jackets, to help keep our clients safe and happy on the trip. We provide meals on most river trips which take up 1 or more full days.
